Euclid: The Game - Tutorial

Goal:

  1. Construct a line segment from A to B.
  2. Construct a ray from C to D.
  3. Construct a circle with center E and radius EF.
  4. Construct a circle with center F and radius EF.
  5. Construct a (black) point at the intersection of AB and the ray CD.

Hint: Hold your mouse on a tool to get a brief instruction how the tool works.

Well done ! You are now ready to begin with Level 1
